### Comparative form
1 -> (i)er... than
- My sister is older than me
- Im happier than you

2 -> more...than
- Could you speak more slowly please?

3 -> (not) as...as
- She is just as smart as he

### Superlative form
1 -> the ...(i)est
- Johon is the nicest person i know
- Joana is the happiest student in the class
