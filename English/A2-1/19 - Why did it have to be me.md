### Wh questions
We use question words to ask certain types of questions in more detail

	What -> things or actions
	When -> time
	Where -> place
	Who -> people (subject)
	Whom -> people (object)
	Which -> choice / options
	Whose -> possessive
	Why -> reasos
	How -> methods
